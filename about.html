Note from the Founder

At 20 I asked myself, 'why?'. Silence prevailed in the air. I closed my eyes and took a deep breath. The latter was the journey to find the inner me...

I started to create some handmade scribbles to relieve the heavyness of my mind. But surprisingly I got more interest in it. I reffered online resourses as well to accompany my creative mind. And soon victory came to my path. Some of my creative friends also joined me in my journey. It was the birth of so called craft store, The SEVENTH HEAVEN. We named it so bacause we found our immence sense for happiness in it.

Now 3 winters are past. They witnessed our rise as the best creative team in Mid-Guard. We no more relay on online ideas,instead we have made a trend of our own. Our works include Paper Crafts for Home Decor, Gift Cards and Gift Boxes, Paintings, Other Home Decors including wood works, glass works etc.

Thank you for believing in US.

-Team SEVENTH HEAVEN
